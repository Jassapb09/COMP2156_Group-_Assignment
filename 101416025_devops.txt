Dive deep into the world of DevOps with COMP 2156 - DevOps course at George Brown College. This course covers a wide range of topics, including version control, continuous integration, cloud computing, and more. Students will learn how to leverage DevOps practices to streamline software development processes, increase deployment frequency, and enhance overall product quality. Through hands-on labs and real-world projects, students will gain practical experience in implementing DevOps tools and techniques, preparing them for success in today's fast-paced tech industry.
