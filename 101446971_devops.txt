Immerse yourself in the world of DevOps with COMP 2156 - DevOps course at George Brown College. This course introduces students to the principles and practices of DevOps, covering topics such as version control, continuous integration, and deployment automation. Through hands-on labs and real-world projects, students gain practical experience in implementing DevOps tools and techniques, preparing them for success in today's fast-paced tech industry. Join us and discover how DevOps can revolutionize the way you develop, deploy, and manage software.
