Ready to take your skills to the next level? COMP 2156 - DevOps course at George Brown College is your gateway to the world of DevOps. This course covers a wide range of topics, including version control, continuous integration, infrastructure as code, and more. Through hands-on labs and real-world projects, students will gain practical experience in implementing DevOps principles and practices. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, COMP 2156 offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to help you succeed in the dynamic field of DevOps.
